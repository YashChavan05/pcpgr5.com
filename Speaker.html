Speaker

  • Speakers are used to connect to a computer to generate sound, which are one of the most common output devices.
  • Computer speakers are speakers sold for use with computers,although usually capable of other audio uses, eg. for an MP3 player.
  • Most such speaker have an internal amplifier and consequently require a power source,which may be by a mains power supply often via an AC adapter,batteries or a USB port.
  • The signal input connector is often a 3.5mm jack plug colour coded lime green per the RCA connectors are sometimes used,and USB port may supply both signal and power
