TROUBLESHOOTING DEVICES

  1. TROUBLESHOOTING OF KEYBOARD:

    2. PROBLEM SYMPTOM PRELIMINARY CHECKS
    No Key Respond If depressing the keys cause no response
    1. Disconnect the keyboard
      and check the cable for
      continuity.
    2. Observe the corrosion or
      broken pins.
    3. Clean the keys with a tuner
      cleaning spray.
    4. Else replace the keyboard.
    5. If keyboard is wireless,
      check the battery.
    Keyboard won't respond at all or
    Wrong charachter is produced    		 No display responds
    Wrong charachter displays
    One or few keys won't work. The boot up operation occurs properly,
    with correct display action, but when key is pressed no display response.
    Unwanted repeat action. When key is pressed more than one

  2. TROUBLESHOOTING OF MOUSE:

    • Inspect the mouse for hardware damage.
    • Clean the mouse.
    • Replace the batteries.
    • Try different USB ports.
    • Connect the mouse directly to the USB port.
    • Use the ouse on a appropriate surface.
    • Update the drives.
      •

  3. TROUBLESHOOTING OF MICROPHONE:

    4. SYMPTOM/PROBLEM PRELIMINARY CHECKS
    Unwanted Noise / Whizzing / Buzzing
    1. Check the cable.
    2. Check the Microphone.
    3. Check the Jack for Polarity.
    No audio
    1. Check the cable continuity.
    2. Check the Speaker functionality.
    3. Check the Audio drives.

  4. TROUBLESHOOTING OF WEBCAM:

    • Check your Antivirus settings.
    • Plug the webcam into different computer.
    • Check the device connection.
    • Check the USB port.
    • Make sure correct device is enabled.
    • Consult the manufacturer.
    • Update Webcam drives.
    • Change your webcam system settings.
      •

  5. TROUBLESHOOTING OF MONITOR:

    6. SYMPTOMS PRELIMINARY CHECKS
    No picture or monitor blank/
    1. Check the power card.
    2. In case of buzzing sound the monitor is not connected properly to video card.
    3. Adjust the brightness and contrast.
    4. Check the video error codes or beeps but power on self test(POST).
    Blank monitor after restart.
    1. Check the power card.
    2. Disconnect the other peripherals except monitor, keyboard, mouse and reboot.
    Monitor displays the wavy or curvy lines.
    1. Verify the refresh rate and resolution settings.
    Colour patches on screen.
    1. Reboot in safe mode.
    2. Reconfigure the monitor using computer management option in device manager.
    Blurred display.
    1. Verify the video card and its pins.

  6. TROUBLESHOOTING OF PRINTER:

    7. SYMPTOMS PRELIMINARY CHECKS
    White strike on document.
    1. Clean the cartidge with cotton swab using isoprophyl alcohol.
    Connectivity problem, Test page not working.
    1. Reconfiguration of printer.

  7. TROUBLESHOOTING OF SCANNER:
