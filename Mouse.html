Mouse
  • A mouse is a small hardware input device used by hand.
  • It controls the movement of the cursor on the computer screen and allows users to move and select folders, text, files, and icons on a computer.
  • It is an object, which needs to put on a hard-flat surface to use.
  • When the users move the mouse, the cursor moves in the same direction on the display screen.
  • The mouse invention is considered as one of the most important breakthroughs in the computer field as it helps to reduce the use of a keyboard.
